Getting to that next level

In Software Programming getting the basic knowledge can be easy. But where do we go from there. That can be the challenge. How do you get froman1 Getting that first job with the knowledge that you have attained, to becoming that Senior Developer. But before you can do that, what do you really want to do?

Navigating the development world can be really confusing. Some of the more common job titles include:

  • Front-End Developer
  • Backend Developer
  • Web Developer
  • Mobile Developer
  • Ruby-on-Rails Developer
  • JavaScript Developer
  • ... And the list can go on

Here at CodeMentor, our goal is get you from Point A to Point B and encompass all the things in between.

First, we start with the interviewe prep, resume review and work shops that strengthen the knowledge that you already have. We will use common technologies, and monthly projects that we will improve week in and week out to really stregnthen your programming skills. Not too mention that it will be nice to have on your GitHub profile.

Then, once you have your foot in the door, we will continue to do workshops, virtually or in person, that will continue to strengthen your skills. By partnering with our team we can really dive into what exactly your interests are, or the technologies that your using everday at your job.

Finally, during this we can have more discussion on what you think a Senior level devleoper is, and look further at job descriptions to get you too that level.

Ready to begin, contact our team and lets dive in to some great code!
